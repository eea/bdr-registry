Reporting requirements and forms

From this year the EEA is using online webforms to collect the data you need to submit. This allows amongst others the data to be fed directly into a database without the need for manual processing. The webforms can perform certain checks immediately while you are completing the form or immediately after submission. This will increase the user friendliness and reduce the administrative burden for all stakeholders. It will also reduce the number of questions posed to you after submission. While the webforms by nature will look somewhat different from the Excel sheets used so far, no additional data will be requested compared to previous years. Due to the functionality of the online forms it was possible to eliminate certain tables and to avoid that data needs to be repeated in two different forms. For example companies importing ODS and using them for feedstock data had to fill in certain data in both forms in the past which was an additional source of errors.

Reporting is not required if you solely import or export products or equipments containing or relying on ODS (e.g. fire extinguishers or aircraft). Reporting is not required if you produce, import or export less than one tonne of fluorinated greenhouse gases.
