Self registration

Reporting undertakings

Designated reporters of an undertaking, who have to report in accordance with Article 6 of the F-Gas Regulation or Article 27 of the ODS Regulation, should register in order to be able to report.

  • In case you or another person in your company already have received a username and password but have forgotten it or in case you need further assistance logging in, please contact the helpdesk at: BDR.helpdesk@eea.europa.eu.
  • In case your undertaking is a first time reporter and has therefore not yet received a username and password, please proceed to the self-registration form below.

Self-registration form

Please note that this registration form should only be used if your company has not yet received a login and a password. In the case of a forgotten login or password or in the case of a change of reporter details, please contact the helpdesk at: BDR.helpdesk@eea.europa.eu.

For undertakings reporting for the first time, the account request will be evaluated and login and password will be provided.
